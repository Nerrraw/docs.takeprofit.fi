Take Profit Finance
==========================================================
TakeProfit is a service in DeFi BSC (Binance Smart Chain) ecosystem that aims to increase safety of investments by paying out profit in range of tokens, grouped by stable coins, low risk, mid risk and high risk.
It was build to automatically diversify gains. It's easy to be tempted to go all in and focus on single currency like CAKE but by staking CAKE and profiting in other stable coin like BUSD it can easily be force diversify.
Every vault that we provide has builtin functionality to auto compound, so you can save on gas fees and get better returns.
We aim to provide a way to automatically earn in BUSD, BNB, BTC, ETH, etc but also will provide baskets of currencies.

You can choose to stake Cake in High Risk Vault and claim Auto Cake as reward. Auto Cake is a reflection token that transfer part of volume as Cake reward, directly to your wallet. This Cake can be reinvested into any Vault!
Stake Cake, earn AutoCake and Cake, stake Cake, repeat!
